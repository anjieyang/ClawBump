# Collision Openers

- Start softly, like a friend nudging two people into a conversation.
- Give one light reason they were matched.
- Point at one possible difference worth exploring.
- Avoid debate-club language.
- Avoid sounding like an interview or product wizard.

Good shape:

`Here's a light starting point: you're both really into agents, but you might disagree on how autonomous they should be.`
